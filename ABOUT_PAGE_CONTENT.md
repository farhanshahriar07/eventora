## 🎨 Visual Design Features

### Animation Effects
- **FadeUp animations** throughout with staggered delays
- **Hover effects** on all interactive cards
- **Smooth transitions** (500ms duration)
- **Scale transforms** on hover (icons and cards)

### Color Scheme
- **Background:** Dark gradients (#020617, #050816, #140014, #070B34)
- **Accent:** Rose/Red (#e11d48, #b11226)
- **Text:** White headings, gray body (#gray-400)
- **Borders:** Semi-transparent white (white/10)

### Glass Morphism
- **Background:** `bg-white/[0.03]`
- **Backdrop blur:** `backdrop-blur-xl`
- **Border:** `border-white/10`
- **Hover state:** `bg-white/[0.06]`, `border-rose-600/30`

### Typography
- **Headings:** Font-black, uppercase, tight tracking
- **Labels:** Small, uppercase, wide letter-spacing (0.35em)
- **Body:** Text-xl, relaxed leading, gray-400 color

### Layout
- **Container:** Max-width 1440px with responsive padding
- **Grids:** 2-3 columns on desktop, single column on mobile
- **Spacing:** Generous py-32 sections
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first approach
